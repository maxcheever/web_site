i am an artist concerned with creative process, originality, computational idealism, and existence within digital culture. My research interests include modern, post-modern, & contemporary art (particularly the avant-garde or lack thereof), computer vision & computer graphics, digital culture, non-exhaustive lists, and comma splices, I currently study art, art history, computer science, and mathematics at the pennsylvannia state university.

my works include art for everyone, tools for artists, and writing for no one. feel free to contact me through whichever avenue you prefer. i always try to be open to new ideas, collaboration, advice, and criticism.

this web site serves an archive for my work, and it is meant to present as accessible and be perceived as native to a digital existence.
